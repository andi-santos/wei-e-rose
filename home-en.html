The "White Rose" is the symbol of a unified resistance against racism, disinformation, discrimination and hatred. It is a free symbol for all those who choose to rebel and stand together under the sign of humanity.

Sophie Scholl, Hans Scholl and Alexander Schmorell rebelled in times of hatred and violence and as a consequence, were killed for their beliefs by the Nazi regime. We want to keep their legacy alive and make their symbol available to all those who work for intercultural and interpersonal connectedness. This time does not need heroes; it needs people, just like us.

Everybody is allowed to use this symbol. It cannot be misused because it both belongs to no one and everyone. It is the manifestation of an idea and a movement. Therefore, we provide this symbol here as an invitation for you to share it and to use it in the sense of unity, understanding, enlightenment and love.

We will use this website exclusively to make the symbol of the "White Rose" available and to pass it on in the aforementioned sense. Â
